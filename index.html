Life and career of koffee

Mikayla Simpson was born in feb16 2000. Her music insipration steemed
from her mother's religious background, who was a seventh day adventist.
At the age of 12 she taught herself guitar and began writting lyrics
inspired by listening to reggae singer protoje.
2016 she won her school talent show without realizing that the show she performed was an audition.
she kept on learning music and in 2017 she released hr first single "legend"
which became a viral instagram hit.
2018 she was invited to join chronixx on a BBC Radio 1Xtra broadcast and also went on a tour with him in UK.
october 2020 koffee was named by Elle magazine of "10 trailblazing wmen changing the future you need to know"
Koffee kept growing in music till date (2021) where she has released her recent song West indies.
